Our Mission

To engineer a champion Micromouse through superior design and intelligent strategy.

Our project's mission is to design, build, and program a fully autonomous "Micromouse" capable of dominating the Gujarat Robofest 5.0 Maze Solver challenge. The primary objective is twofold: first, to autonomously explore and create a perfect map of an unknown maze. Second, to analyze this map to calculate the absolute shortest path and then execute a flawless, high-speed run to the destination. Success is defined by achieving the fastest possible time while adhering strictly to all competition rules, showcasing a perfect blend of speed, intelligence, and reliability.
